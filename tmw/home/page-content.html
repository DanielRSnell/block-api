Texas Made, Uncompromising Quality.

Weld Tables, Done Right.

You won't find a better combination of features, strength, versatility and value anywhere!

  • US Manufacturing

    Manufactured entirely in Texas, just north of Houston, by certified US welders. Feel free to visit!

  • North American Material

    Our steel comes from only North American facilities. Primarily plate from the US, and tubing from Mexico.

  • ISO 9001 Certified

    Our facility and processes are ISO 9001:2015 certified by independent auditors.

  • High Grade Steel

    We don’t cut corners! A572-50 is our standard! Our competitors use cheap imported A36 or lower grade steel.

  • Exclusive Features

    From our integrated Super Slots, to our under-table mounting system, no one offers more flexibility and customization.

  • Unlimited Scaleability

    Our welding tables can be connected together on every side to easily expand in any direction.
